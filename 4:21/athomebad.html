Take a Walk

SCROLL

It's easy to feel trapped, helpless, and suffocated when seeing a loved one suffer from cancer.

Many studies prove that walking and physical exercise produces molecules that nourish brain activity through increased oxygen intake and allow us to feel more at ease.

In addition, taking a walk outside is an easy way to remind yourself that there is a larger world around you - taking you out of the space of your loved one's cancer treatment just for a moment.
