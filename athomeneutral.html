Explore a Passion

SCROLL

As caregivers, we devote ourselves to our loved ones, forgetting to take time to ourselves. Is there a hobby you've always enjoyed or hoped to pursue?

Whether it's baking, knitting, or even reading a new book, take a moment out of your day to pay attention to these passions.

Even if you only have five minutes to do this, setting aside time for your own interests is an effective way to mentally separate yourself from the exhaustions of cancer. This hobby can be a resourceful destination for you throughout your journey.
