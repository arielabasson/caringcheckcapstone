Cancer is not only extremely mentally tolling on its victims but also their caretakers. It is often easy for us to opt to neglect to consider the detrimental effects that cancer has on caretakers.

As caretakers, we may be prone to solely placing an emphasis on our loved one’s health and forget about our own. Sometimes, this can be attributed to lack of time to consider our well-being or lack of means.

“Take Five” is an online check-in platform meant to prompt caretakers to frequently pause, take five, and consider their current states of mind through quick and accessible exercises.

Take a Few is a mobile interactive experience designed by Ariela Basson. The goal of Take a Few is to provide friends, family, and caregivers of cancer patients with an accessible and quick moment to themselves. To experience Take a Few in it's full capacity, please visit from your mobile device.
