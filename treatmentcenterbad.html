Talk it Out

SCROLL

Are there others around you? Are you in a waiting room?

If so, take a moment to introduce yourself to someone new and strike up a conversation. At an Ohio State University breast cancer oncology waiting room, women created a nurturing community for not only patients, but also their loved ones.

Ask someone how their day is going? How are they feeling? Even if the conversation does not directly pertain to your loved one's treatment, at least you can distract through communication and conversation.
